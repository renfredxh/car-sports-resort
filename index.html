Car Sports Resort

  • High Concept: Play as a sports car on the tennis court of a sports resort.
  • Player Experience Goals: Freedom of choice.
  • Genre: Racing/sports
  • Game play: Player can use the up, down, left, and right arrow keys to move the car. The player can try to hit the tennis ball as it moves and hear a satisfying sound, or they can not hit it. It's completely up to them. There are no points. The ball will accelerate as the game goes on and if it hits the bottom of the court the game will reset.
  • Features: Open-ended racing and sports game.
  • Setting/Premise: A sports car who enjoys sports is visiting a sports resort to play tennis.
  • Target Audience: People who enjoy sports, or cars, or both
  • Play time: As long as the player wants to.
  • Strategies: None.
  • Prototype Assets: Tennis court, ball, and car from online sprite resources.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Mario Tennis.
